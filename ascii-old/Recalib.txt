Recalibrated Radiocarbon Assays
Radiocarbon assays in Chapter 5 were calibrated with CALIB 4.4, based on the
INTCAL98 terrestrial database. In June, 2006, the assays were recalibrated using the
newer version 5.02, based on the INTCAL04 database, with the results shown below. The
chief effect of the new calibration is to reduce the uncertainty somewhat. Six of the
assays have slightly younger calibrated dates, and five have slightly older dates. The only
calibrated date that is changed much is BETA 73471, which becomes slightly older.
